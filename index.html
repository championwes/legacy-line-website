Who We Are

A Texas Brokerage Built on a Handshake Standard

Legacy Line Fr8 & Logistix LLC is a freight brokerage headquartered in Tomball, Texas, on the north side of the Houston metro — one of the busiest freight corridors in the country. We hold active federal broker authority and operate interstate, arranging truckload and LTL capacity for shippers who want their freight handled by people, not portals.

The name is the mission: move every load in a way that builds a legacy — for our shippers, our carriers and our family business.
